College Style Chili

Serves 4-5 people

Ingredients:

  • 1 white onion coarsely chopped
  • 1-2 bell peppers coarsely chopped
  • 2 15oz cans of tomatoes
  • 2-3 15oz cans of refried beans
  • 1lb of gound meat
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 15oz can of corn
  • 2-3 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 2 teaspoons of black pepper
  • 1-2 tablespoons of cumin
  • 1-2 teaspoons of powdered garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of salt (or salt to desired taste)

Directions:

  • In a non-stick pan, heat 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil on medium heat.
  • Add the meat and cook until browned.
  • Drain any remaining fat.
  • In a large pot, add the meat and the rest of the ingredients and stir to mix.
  • Let simmer uncovered on a low heat for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  • Let it continue to simmer for additional time to thicken it to desired amount.
